All financing programs

Aboriginal Business and Entrepreneurship Development

Grant or contribution | Indigenous and Northern Affairs Canada
Up to $250,000
You can apply for funding for your Aboriginal-business for business development support for business planning, expansion, new product development, technology, marketing and other operating costs.

Advance Payments Program

Loan guarantee | Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada
Up to $400,000
You could get up to 50% of the average market price of your stored crops or unsold livestock as a cash advance. You need to repay your cash advance as your products are sold and you need to repay the total amount within 18 months (up to 24 months for cattle and bison).

AgriInnovation Program

Grant or contribution | Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada
Up to $10 million
Access funding for research and development activities that bring innovation to the agriculture and agri-food sector; and those that help industry bring the results of research and development to market through adoption/commercialization.

Alberta Women Entrepreneurs

Loan | Western Economic Diversification Canada
Up to $150,000
Access loans for your Alberta woman-owned business based on an assessment of the viability of your business plan and considering the economic impact of the business in terms of job creation and future growth.

Apprenticeship Job Creation Tax Credit

Tax credit | Canada Revenue Agency
Up to $2000/year per apprentice
If you hire an apprentice, you could get a tax credit equal to 10% of that person's salary.

Aquaculture Collaborative Research and Development Program

Grant or contribution | Fisheries and Oceans Canada
Up to $10 million
By collaborating with government researchers, you could increase your business' scientific capacity for projects relating to fish health and environmental performance.

Atlantic Innovation Fund

Grant or contribution | Atlantic Canada Opportunities Agency
$500,000 to $3 million
Access funding to develop and commercialize new technologies in Atlantic Canada.

Automotive Innovation Fund

Grant or contribution | Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada
Up to $500 million
If you have an incorporated automotive business, you could get funding to build greener, more fuel-efficient vehicles through innovation and R&D.

BDC Business Loans

Loans | Business Development Bank of Canada
Various amounts
Access loans to expand production, purchase new equipment or finance a special project. Loans of up to $100,000 are available online within 5 business days.

Canada Summer Jobs

Wage subsidy | Service Canada
Up to 50% of hourly wage
If you hire students for the summer, this subsidy could help you cover up to half of their wages.
